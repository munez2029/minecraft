Why Minecraft?

Creativity - Minecraft isn't about mining or crafting its not even about grinding its about creativity
Minecraft teaches us that the only limit is ones creativity.

Teamwork - Minecraft teaches us teamwork like in bedwars
someone has to defend, someone has to attack and someone has to get resources as my friend says
“The more we mine the stronger our trust binds”.

Having fun - Lastly having fun the most important part of every game Minecraft teaches as
to have fun by giving challenges giving multiplayer to connect with your friends Minecraft gives
every opportunity to have fun.
